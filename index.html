  • Reviewing tapes of 1st-lien, business-purpose NPLs secured by NOO residential properties

  • Managing the transaction through a structured process from first discussion to close

Our Approach

  • Clear communication and organized intake from the start
  • Disciplined screening to reduce friction and wasted time
  • Professional coordination across participants and materials
  • Reliable process support from initial discussion through completion

Every engagement is approached with careful planning, clear communication, and disciplined execution — so both parties feel aligned and confident in the process.

What We Review

To keep conversations efficient, we prioritize tapes that match the following profile:

Asset Profile

  • 1st-lien positions
  • Business-purpose loans
  • NOO residential collateral
  • Residential investment properties (SFR / MF)
  • Max LTV of 75%

Loan Status

  • NPL / non-performing positions
  • $500k – $3mm typical loan size
  • Portfolios or single assets
  • Nationwide consideration (tape-driven)
  • Clear data points = faster review

Helpful Tape Fields

  • Property address / collateral type
  • UPB, note rate, maturity
  • Payment history / delinquency status
  • Occupancy, valuation / BPO, legal status

If you’re unsure whether a tape fits, send a high-level summary — we’ll quickly confirm alignment.
